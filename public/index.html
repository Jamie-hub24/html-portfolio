Paul's Favourite Movies

My Top 10 Movies of All Time (in no particular order)

The Tracey Fragments

An experimental indie film about a young girl who's life has been shattered by her brother's disappearance.

Ginger Snaps

A Canadian horror film about two sisters who are obsessed with death and the macabre. A visceral exploration of becoming an adult.

The Tree of life

A beautiful and poetic film about the meaning of life and the universe by visionary director Terence Malik.

Withnail and I

A British cult classic about two struggling actors who go on a disastrous holiday by mistake.

Kundun

A biographical film about the life of the 14th Dalai Lama, directed by Martin Scorsese.

Civil War

A sci-fi film directed by Alex Garland about how the media turning politics into a divisive spectacle can lead to the downfall of society.

The Dreamers

A film about three young film students living in Paris on the eve of the 1968 Paris student riots.

The Thin Red Line

A war film directed by Terence Malik about the battle of Guadalcanal during World War II.

Rashomon

A Japanese film directed by Akira Kurosawa about the nature of truth and perspective.

Master and Commander: The Far Side of the World

A historical drama about a British naval captain during the Napoleonic Wars, directed by Peter Weir.
